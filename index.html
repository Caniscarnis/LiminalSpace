    • What are liminal spaces?

    Liminal Space is a popularized internet aesthetic based on imagery assosciated with emptiness and pictures of places that invoke surrealism. It is often empty, and completly devoid of people The term liminal spaces comes from the latin "limen" meaning threshold. It also comes from the term 'liminality' which is the state of transition or of beeing between.
